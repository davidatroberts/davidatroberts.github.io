About Me

I’m a Senior Research Software Engineer living in Newcastle upon Tyne.

I completed a PhD in Computer Science at Durham University in 2016. My main research areas included volume rendering and stereoscopic displays.

On this site you’ll find a random assortment of my projects, bits of code and reviews of interesting things I’ve picked up.
